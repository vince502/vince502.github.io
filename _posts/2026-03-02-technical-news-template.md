For concise and high-signal news coverage, each brief follows this structure:

- **Change summary:** what shipped and where it applies.
- **Technical impact:** how teams may need to adapt architecture or delivery.
- **Assessment:** my interpretation of long-term significance.
- **References:** official release notes, documentation, or source material.
